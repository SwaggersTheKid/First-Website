White Rice

    Steps of Cooking White Rice
  • Rinse the Rice in water
  • Grab a pot and fill it with enough water to reach the height of the Rice
  • Drop a small cube of butter into the pot and add a small amount of oil
  • Grab a rice spoon and mix the oil and butter with the rice and mix it every 3 minutes or so and repeat this for about 30 minutes
  • if theirs still water in the pot wait until all the water is evaporated in the pot
  • The rice should be chewy and steaming with a nice smell
  • You can now eat rice
